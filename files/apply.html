Application Procedure

Interested in the nursery school? Here is information on how to apply and what to do once accepted, as well as procedures for re-enrollment.

If you are looking to enroll in Bear Tracks, please navigate to the Bear Tracks page under "Classrooms".


Applying Students

1. Visit

Parents interested in Washington University Nursery School should call the school to schedule a visit.

Call: 314-935-6689
6926 Forest Park Parkway, St. Louis, MO 63130


2. Submit Application

Complete the Application for Admission form, which is available to fill out online or can be printed and returned to the school as a PDF.

Application for Admission (Online) Application for Admission (PDF)

A $40 application fee must be submitted along with your application for admission.


3. Enrollment Notification

After your form and fee are received, your child is placed on an enrollment list. You will be notified of acceptance in the March preceding the fall enrollment date. 5. Orientation

This visiting day is usually the day after Labor Day and gives the child and parent(s) an opportunity to visit the classroom and meet the teachers and classmates. This visit is one hour. It will allow time for the child to enjoy free play and also gives parent(s) a brief overview of what to expect during the school year. The next day, a child will attend school on his/her own (either Wednesday or Thursday, see orientation schedule). The class is divided in half, attending on either Wednesday or Thursday. This schedule allows the children and teachers to interact with a small group before the entire class attends on Friday.
